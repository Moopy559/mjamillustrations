About Me

My name is Maddi Jamieson, I'm a Brisbane/Yuggera based artist who loves to create stylized illustrations of women, with the occasional creature or animal thrown into the mix. After discovering the joys of watercolour and gouache when I was a teenager, I love creating works that are colourful, bright and filled with attitude.I love to try out new drawing styles. My works are often 'thematic' based off my interests and mood at the time, and this is reflected in the different subjects and colours I experiment with.

To see the bulk of my Illustration work, the best place to find me is Instagram.
